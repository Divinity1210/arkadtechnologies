About Arkad

Building for the moments where traditional systems break down

Arkad Technologies is a fintech and technology company building practical financial infrastructure and products that help people and businesses move money, access responsible credit, and manage cross-border life with clarity and control.

We focus on the moments where traditional systems break down: funding deadlines that don't match cashflow, essentials that can't wait, payouts that arrive late, trade payments that get stuck in conversion, and global spending that comes with hidden friction.
